DINI - Do I Need It?

Privacy Policy

DINI - Do I Need It? ("the App") helps users practice intentional spending by adding items to a wishlist with a cooldown period. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers. All data you enter (items, prices, notes, history) is stored locally on your device via Apple's on-device storage.

2. How Your Data Is Used

Since your data remains on your device, we do not use, share, or sell your information. Local notifications are used solely to alert you when a cooldown ends.

3. Third-Party Services

The current version of the App does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. If this changes in future versions, this policy will be updated accordingly.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained locally until you delete it or uninstall the App. Uninstalling the App will permanently remove all stored data from your device.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect or maintain information about children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
